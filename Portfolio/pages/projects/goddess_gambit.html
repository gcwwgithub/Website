The Goddess's Gambit (2023)
Role: Gameplay Programmer
Team Size: 1
Project Length: 6 weeks
Engine: Unity
Language: C#
Created for: Digipen Level Design
Game Description
The Goddess's Gambit is a 2D roguelike dungeon crawler where you play as a ordinary man Bob who got transported to another world by a goddess to defeat the demon king. Using your newly acquired magic wand, escape the dungeon you got transported in so you can start your journey to defeat the demon king.
Project Role
This is a solo project where I created a procedural dungeon algorithm and also all the PCG elements that comes with it. I also made the gameplay systems for the game and various story elements. Lastly, all art assets were drawn by me.
