Fired + L + Ratio (2022)
Role: Designer
Team Size: 4
Project Length: 4 weeks
Created for: Digipen Introduction to Game Design
Game Description
Fired + L + Ratio is a solo player card game where the goal is to ensure you as a fresh faced programmer have a work life balance and not get burnt out. Survive all the way with your sanity intact and your job secure.
Project Role
As a designer, I worked with my team to come up with the game mechanics and rules of the game. I also helped to plan out the cards that players will use in the game.
