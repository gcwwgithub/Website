A Night to Remember (2023)
Role: Level Designer & Programmer
Team Size: 1
Project Length: 6 weeks
Engine: Unity
Language: C#
Created for: Digipen Level Design
Game Description
A Night To Remember is a 3D walking simulator where you play as a librarian working overtime and mysterious circumstances start to occur around you. Investigate and explore the level to solve the mysteries.
Project Role
This is a solo project where I made 3 different separate environments, a modern library, an underground archive and a mysterious grotto and also scripted all the events connecting these environments together.
