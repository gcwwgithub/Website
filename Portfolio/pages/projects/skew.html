SKEW
Role: Producer & Gameplay Programmer
Team Size: 16
Project Length: 7 months
Engine: Custom
Language: C# C++
Created for: Digipen Software Engineering Project 5/6
Introduction
Skew is a first person hack and slash game where you play as Beatrice who is a chef turned prisoner of the colony’s tyrant.
Contributions
This game was done for Software Engineering Project 6 where I worked in a team of 15 people to develop the game and the custom engine to run the game. My role was the Producer of the group where I led, managed and keep track of every team members progress to ensure all timeline and goals are met. As a gameplay programmer, I was in charge of creating the overall level systems and game flow. I also scripted the cutscenes and event system.
