ARID: A Rumble in the Desert (2022)
Role: Designer
Team Size: 4
Project Length: 6 weeks
Created for: Digipen Introduction to Game Design
Game Description
Arid is a solo player tabletop board game where you play as the hero who must slay the Worm who will destroy the world in 8 days. Collect resources and once you have the strength required to kill the worm, chase after it and defeat it so that the world will be saved. Be careful though, you do not have a lot of time to do so...
Project Role
As a designer, I worked with my team to come up with the game mechanics and rules of the game. I also helped to plan out the game board and the cards that players will use in the game.
