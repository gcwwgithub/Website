Waterslide VR (2018)
Role:Project Lead & Gameplay Programmer
Team Size: 5
Project Length: 3 months
Engine: Unity
Language: C#
Created for: Temasek Polytechnic Major Project
Game Description
Waterslide VR is a VR simulation game made in collaboration with Wild Wild Wet featured at Temasek Polytechnic's InfoTech Day 2019 and Downtown East 30th Anniversary. In this game, you get to experience a VR experience of riding a water slide modelled after the slides in Wild Wild Wet. Play mini games while travelling down the slide or play at the carnival games in the virtual water park and have a enjoyable time.
Project Role
As the Project Lead, I was responsible for managing the team and ensuring that the project was completed on time. Additionally, I served as the main gameplay programmer for the project, implementing the water slide simulation and the different slide games that the player can play while sliding down the water slide.
